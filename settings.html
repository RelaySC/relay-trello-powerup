You can update the settings for the Relay power-up below.

"On-Hold" Threshold

You must specify the percentage from 0 to 100.

This will amount of board members required to disapprove an idea before it is placed in "On-Hold" status.

"Scrapped" Threshold

You must specify the percentage from 0 to 100.

This will amount of board members required to disapprove an idea before it is placed in "Scrapped" status.
